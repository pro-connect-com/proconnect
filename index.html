About ProConnect Kenya

Founded in Nairobi in 2022, ProConnect Kenya is dedicated to transforming the tech employment landscape across Kenya by connecting verified professionals with transparent opportunities.

We believe every Kenyan tech professional deserves fair access to quality jobs with clear compensation expectations.

Our Mission:

To eliminate information asymmetry in Kenya's tech job market through verification and transparency.
