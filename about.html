About ProConnect Kenya

Building Kenya's most trusted tech professional network

Our Kenyan Story

Founded in Nairobi in 2022, ProConnect Kenya was born from a simple observation: Kenya's booming tech sector lacked transparency in hiring and career growth.

We set out to create a platform where Kenyan tech professionals could connect with opportunities based on verified skills and clear salary expectations.

Our Vision:

To become Kenya's most trusted tech professional network by 2025.

Our Mission

To transform Kenya's tech employment landscape through:

Verification

M-Pesa and ID verification for all Kenyan members

Transparency

Mandatory salary ranges on all Kenyan job posts

Community

500+ vetted Kenyan tech professionals
